How It Works

  1. Enter your current share quantity and average price.
  2. Enter the amount you want to invest and the buying rate.
  3. Add any brokerage/transaction charges (if any).
  4. Click "Calculate New Average" to see results.

Formula: New Avg = (Current Total Cost + New Purchase Amount + Brokerage) / (Current Qty + New Qty)

Example from your data:

  • Current: 6,230 shares @ 25.50 avg
  • Buy: 25,194 TK @ 24.70 rate
  • Brokerage: 125.97 TK
