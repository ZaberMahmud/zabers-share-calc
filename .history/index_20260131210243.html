How It Works

Learn about each calculator and see examples

Average Calculator

Calculate your new average price after buying more shares.

Formula: New Avg = (Current Cost + New Amount + Brokerage) ÷ (Current Qty + New Qty)

Example: EBL Purchase

  • Current: 6,230 shares @ 25.50Tk
  • New Buy: 25,194Tk @ 24.70Tk per share
  • Brokerage: 125.97Tk
  • Result: 7,250 shares @ 25.40Tk average

Quantity Calculator

Find how many shares you can buy with a specific amount.

Formula: Shares = Floor(Amount ÷ Price)
Avg Cost = (Amount + Brokerage) ÷ Shares

Example: MATINSPINN

  • Amount: 73,220Tk
  • Price: 52.30Tk per share
  • Brokerage: 366.10Tk (0.5%)
  • Result: 1,400 shares @ 52.56Tk average

Bonus/Dividend Calculator

Calculate cash dividends and bonus shares.

Dividend: Gross = Shares × (Div% × Face Value)
Net = Gross × (1 - Tax%)
Bonus: Bonus = Current × (Ratio A ÷ Ratio B)
New Avg = (Current × Avg) ÷ Total

Example: MATINSPINN Dividend

  • Shares: 1,400
  • Dividend: 35% of 10Tk
  • Tax: 15%
  • Result: 4,900Tk gross, 4,165Tk net

Gain/Loss Calculator

Calculate unrealised profit or loss on your investments.

Formulas: Current Value = Shares × Market Price
Gain/Loss = Current Value - Total Cost
% Change = (Gain/Loss ÷ Total Cost) × 100

Example: MATINSPINN Loss

  • Shares: 1,400
  • Avg Price: 52.56Tk
  • Market Price: 47.60Tk
  • Result: 6,946.10Tk loss (-9.44%)
